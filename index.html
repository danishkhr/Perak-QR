How It Works

From Street Corner to
Digital Story

PerakQR turns the lived history of every heritage business into a digital experience — one scan at a time.

01

SME Registers

Heritage business owners — particularly elderly operators — register their shop, stall, or craft with their story and history.

02

Story is Crafted

Our team visits on-ground for a 30-minute interview — documenting the authentic narrative, photos, and cultural significance.

03

QR Goes Live

A unique QR code is placed at the business. Scan it to access the full digital profile — photos, story, and directions.

04

Visitors Discover

Tourists and locals browse PerakQR to plan visits, find hidden gems, and experience Perak through the people who built it.

Digital Storytelling

Not Just a Directory.
A Living Archive.

Every PerakQR listing is a story — the kind that gets lost when a shop closes or an owner passes on. We document what Google Maps can't.

On-ground Story Collection

Our field team conducts real interviews with business owners — capturing voice, history, and sentiment no form can replicate.

Living History That Updates

Owners update their own pages — new dishes, seasonal offerings, apprentice stories — keeping the archive alive and current.

Intergenerational Handover

When the next generation takes over, the story continues — not starts fresh. Built-in succession storytelling for family businesses.

Elderly Programme

Seniors as Protagonists,
Not Beneficiaries

The most valuable heritage in Perak lives in the hands and memories of its elderly entrepreneurs. PerakQR doesn't serve them — it amplifies them.
